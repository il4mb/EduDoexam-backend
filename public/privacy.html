Privacy Policy for EduDoExam

At EduDoExam, your privacy is a priority. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when using our online exam platform with AI expression classification technology.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and other necessary details required for account creation and authentication.
  • Exam Data: Responses, performance data, and timestamps related to exams you take.
  • Camera Data: Video feed during exams to analyze expressions using AI for monitoring purposes.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and network information to ensure compatibility and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide a secure and efficient online examination experience.
  • To analyze expressions during exams and notify teachers of potential concerns or unusual activities.
  • To improve platform features, AI algorithms, and overall user experience.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take the following measures to safeguard your data:

  • Data encryption during transmission and storage.
  • Strict access control to ensure only authorized personnel can access data.
  • Regular audits and security updates to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Teachers/Administrators: Exam results and AI analysis to assist with monitoring.
  • Third-Party Services: Trusted service providers for data storage and AI processing, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for AI-based monitoring, understanding this may limit your ability to use the platform.
  • Request clarification on how your data is used.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or comply with legal obligations.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please review this page periodically for updates.
